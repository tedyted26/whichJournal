A journal and conference finder for new researchers

Are you new to the world of research papers and investigation? Do you feel lost when trying to search for a place to publish? Do you wish to share your work but don't know when or where? This is your place:

Open Access Journals

Journals that offer their contents to the readers for free. To publish in Open Acess Journals, the author has to pay for the administrative costs, (2500€ aprox.), depending on different factors.

Impact factor

Most popular bibliometric indicator used to measure the quality of a journal based off the frequency of citations of said journal's articles during the past two years.

Predatory Journals

Journals that claim to be legitimate but follow dishonest practices. They usually prioritize economic gain over quality, publishing false information, not reviewing their articles, and trying to take advantage of new researchers.

Conferences

Scientific meetings between scientist and researchers of a specific field. They are a very powerful way to share and discuss knowledge about new data or recent developments. New researchers can use them to give visibility to their work.
